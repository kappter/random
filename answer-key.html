📚 General Grading Guidance
Multiple Choice: One correct answer only. Award full marks for the correct letter.
Fill in the Blank: Accept minor spelling variations. The key value or concept must be present.
Short Answer: Award marks for demonstrating understanding of the core concept. Exact wording is not required.
🔬 App Activities: Answers will vary based on the student's run. Evaluate whether the student correctly used the app and interpreted their results. Look for correct identification of patterns, values, or comparisons.
